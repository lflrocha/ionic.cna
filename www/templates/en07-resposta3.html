Brazil

In Legal Forest Reserve areas, the size to be preserved is determined by the biome in which the property is included. Thus, 20% of the rural property areas included in the biomes Pantanal, Atlantic Forest, Caatinga and Pampas (grasslands) should be preserved. In Cerrado (Brazilian savanna) biome, the preservation is of 35% of the areas included in the Legal Amazon and 20% in the other areas. In the Amazon biome, the preservation should be of 80%.

Source: Brazilian Forest Code (Law n. 12.651).
